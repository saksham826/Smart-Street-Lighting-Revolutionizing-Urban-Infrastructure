What is Smart Street Lighting?

Smart street lighting refers to intelligent, energy-efficient systems that adjust based on real-time data, including traffic, weather, and pedestrian movement.

Energy Efficiency

By using LED lights and automated dimming systems, cities can significantly reduce energy consumption and lower their carbon footprints.

Enhanced Security

Smart lights can be integrated with security systems to improve public safety, activating brighter lighting in response to motion detection and emergencies.
